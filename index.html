Who We Are

At Madesan Home Builders Inc., we believe that building a home is more than just construction—it’s about creating a space where families can thrive, memories can be made, and dreams can take shape. Based in Silang, Cavite, we are a trusted name in the construction industry, offering architectural design, high-quality construction services, and comprehensive permit and financing assistance. Whether you’re looking to build your dream home, renovate a property, or invest in a house and lot, our team is committed to delivering excellence, reliability, and craftsmanship in every project. With a strong focus on quality workmanship and customer satisfaction, we use only top-grade materials and provide a 12-month warranty to ensure lasting value. Our flexible payment options—including bank financing, installment plans, and Pag-IBIG loans—make homeownership more accessible and hassle-free. At Madesan Home Builders Inc., we don’t just build houses—we build homes that stand the test of time. Let’s bring your vision to life.
